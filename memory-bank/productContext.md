# Product Context: Lira Mucevherat

## Why This Project Exists
To create a premium digital presence for a heritage jewelry brand that communicates luxury, craftsmanship, and exclusivity.

## Problems It Solves
- Establishes online presence for a traditionally offline luxury business
- Showcases collections with immersive digital experience
- Provides bespoke design request workflow
- Enables appointment scheduling and contact

## User Experience Goals
- Ultra-slow, cinematic animations create a feeling of luxury
- Dark theme with gold accents conveys exclusivity
- Minimal, clean layouts let the content breathe
- Smooth scrolling enhances the premium feel
- Sharp corners (0-4px radius) for modern luxury aesthetic
